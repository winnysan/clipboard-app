Features

Clipboard history

Reuse any item from your clipboard history in a click.

Images support

Copy and paste not just text, but also images.

Pinning items

Save important snippets and access them anytime.

Keyboard shortcuts

Use familiar shortcuts like Control + C and Control + V to speed up your workflow.

Main features

Pinning items

Keep your favorite snippets just a click away. Whether it’s a friendly email greeting, your go-to signature, or a handy code snippet. Pin it and reuse it anytime.

Images support

Clipboard isn’t just for text! You can also copy and store images from the web or other apps. Great for designers, developers, or anyone who works with visual content.

Theme support

Light theme

A clean, eye-friendly look that blends beautifully with your system. Perfect for a bright and focused workspace.

Dark theme

Easy on the eyes in low light. The dark theme turns on with your system's dark mode and helps you stay focused without the glare.
